    📖 Letter from the Editor

    Aria K. Vance · Trade Former Issue #01

    Dear reader,

    Every game you've ever loved began as a wild, improbable idea in someone's head. But for every masterpiece that ships, a hundred brilliant concepts are left on the cutting-room floor. Trade Former is their home.

    We treat these fictional games with the same seriousness as the industry's biggest releases — because imagination is the trade. In this magazine, you'll find detailed blueprints for mechanics that don't exist, lore bibles for worlds that were never rendered, and pitch documents for games that never saw a greenlight.

    We believe that the former future of play is just as important as the one we actually got. Because in every unmade game, there's a lesson, a spark, or a beautiful failure that can inspire what comes next.

    Welcome to the trade.

    — Aria K. Vance · Editor-in-Chief
