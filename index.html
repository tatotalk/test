🎭 Toxic Archetypes

✨ Know thy enemy — these are the toxic personalities you’ll meet in the wild.

👑

The Gaslight King

🔄 Reality Twister

“That never happened.” “You’re too sensitive.” They rewrite history to make you question your own mind.

⚠️ Red flag: You apologize for things you didn’t do.
💡 Survival tip: Keep a journal. Trust your gut, not their version.
🧛

The Energy Vampire

🫠 Drama Magnet

Every conversation is a crisis. They drain your emotional battery and leave you exhausted.

⚠️ Red flag: You feel tired after every interaction.
💡 Survival tip: Set time limits. “I’ve got 10 minutes — what’s up?”
🎯

The Blame Shifter

🤷 Responsibility Dodger

Nothing is ever their fault. It’s the traffic, the weather, your tone — anything but them.

⚠️ Red flag: They never say “I was wrong.”
💡 Survival tip: Use “I” statements. Don’t accept their projections.
📢

The One-Upper

🏆 Competition Addict

Whatever you’ve done, they’ve done it better. Your achievements are a threat to their ego.

⚠️ Red flag: They turn your good news into their story.
💡 Survival tip: Don’t play the game. “That’s great — anyway…”

🛡️ Survival Tactics

⚡ Battle‑tested strategies to protect your peace.

01

Gray Rock

Be boring. Give short, neutral answers. Starve the drama of attention.

02

Broken Record

Repeat your boundary calmly. “I’m not discussing this” ×3

03

Name the Game

Call out the behavior. “It feels like you’re deflecting.” Stops them cold.

04

Exit Strategy

Always have an out. “I have to take this call.” Walk away with dignity.
